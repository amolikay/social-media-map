Global Social Media Usage and Mental Health Impact

A choropleth map showing average daily social media usage hours across countries worldwide. Countries with high usage (6+ hours) are highlighted with red dashed borders.

Data Interpretation:
• Darker orange colors indicate higher daily social media usage
• Red dashed borders highlight countries with concerning usage levels (6+ hours daily)
• Hover over countries to see detailed statistics including mental health and addiction scores
• Gray areas represent countries with no available data
